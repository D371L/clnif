Transparency

Transparency is one of our core values. We operate as a registered non-profit association (Amutah) and are committed to accountability. Our bylaws, annual financial balances, and confirmation of bank account ownership are always available to the public.

Open • Accountable • Trustworthy

Our Approach

We believe that non-profit organizations must be transparent in their governance, finances, and operations. That is why we regularly publish key documents and maintain clear communication with our community and partners.
