About Us

Champions League for Neighborhoods in Football is a registered non‑profit association in Israel. Our mission is to bring young people together from diverse backgrounds and strengthen local communities through football.

Sportsmanship • Inclusion • Empowerment

Who We Are

We are Champions League for Neighborhoods in Football — a movement of community leaders, mentors, volunteers, and football enthusiasts. We believe sport can transform lives and neighborhoods. Beyond the pitch, we build bridges between diverse groups, create safe and welcoming spaces for youth, and open pathways to growth through leagues, city events, skills workshops, and year‑round activities.

Our teams and volunteers work with schools, municipalities, local clubs, and community centers to make participation simple and accessible. Every program is designed to help young people feel seen, supported, and capable of leading positive change around them.

What We Stand For

Champions League for Neighborhoods in Football stands for fairness, respect, and inclusion. Football is our universal language — a platform to practice equality, teamwork, and dialogue. We ensure that every child and teenager, regardless of background, gender, ability, or neighborhood, can step onto the field and feel at home.

We use the game to teach life skills: discipline, perseverance, communication, conflict resolution, and civic engagement. Our vision is simple — stronger youth, stronger neighborhoods, and a culture of belonging that lasts long after the final whistle.
