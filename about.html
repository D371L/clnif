About Us

Champions League for Neighborhoods in Football is a registered non-profit association in Israel. Our mission is to bring young people together from diverse backgrounds and strengthen local communities through football.

Sportsmanship • Inclusion • Empowerment

Who We Are

We are community leaders, volunteers, and football enthusiasts who believe in the power of sports to change lives. By organizing leagues, tournaments, and activities, we create safe spaces for youth to learn, grow, and connect.

What We Stand For

CLNIF is built on fairness, respect, and the idea that football should be accessible to everyone, regardless of background.
